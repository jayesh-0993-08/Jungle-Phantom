## Project Vision

Jungle Phantom is a long-term autonomous UAV project being developed to explore practical applications of robotics, computer vision, and artificial intelligence for **Defence Intelligence, Surveillance & Reconnaissance (ISR).**

The primary objective is to build an autonomous UAV platform capable of operating with minimal human intervention while performing reconnaissance and perception tasks in complex environments.

Although the project's primary focus is defence ISR, the same technologies can also be applied to disaster response, environmental monitoring, wildlife conservation, and search & rescue missions.

This repository documents my engineering journey—from learning the fundamentals to implementing advanced autonomous robotics concepts.

---

# Why this project?

Modern autonomous drones are built by integrating multiple disciplines:

- Robotics
- Computer Vision
- Artificial Intelligence
- Embedded Systems
- Flight Control
- Autonomous Navigation

Instead of learning these topics separately, Jungle Phantom aims to combine them into one practical engineering project.

The goal isn't simply to build another drone.

The goal is to understand **how modern autonomous UAV systems are actually engineered.**
