# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in Jungle Phantom.

This project is currently under active research and development.

At this stage, external code contributions are not being accepted.

However, feedback, discussions, suggestions, and issue reports are always welcome.

If you're interested in collaborating on autonomous UAV systems, robotics, computer vision, or defence-focused research, feel free to open an issue or contact me.

Thank you for your interest in the project.
